Floods: The country experiences both riverine and coastal flooding. Heavy rainfall during typhoons, monsoon seasons, or prolonged periods of rain can result in flash floods and inundation of low-lying areas.

Floods:

- Implement effective flood control measures, such as building dikes, levees, and flood channels.

- Improve urban planning to avoid construction in flood-prone areas.

- Enhance early warning systems and establish community-based flood monitoring.

- Promote reforestation and watershed management to reduce soil erosion and improve water absorption.

Remember, preparedness and awareness are key in minimizing the impact of hazards. Stay informed, follow official guidelines, and be proactive in taking necessary precautions.
