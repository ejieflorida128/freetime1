Landslides: Steep slopes and heavy rainfall make many areas in the Philippines prone to landslides. Deforestation, improper land use, and soil erosion contribute to the vulnerability of these areas.

Landslides:

- Implement slope stabilization measures, such as terracing and retaining walls.

- Promote reforestation and proper land use practices to prevent soil erosion.

- Conduct geohazard assessments and relocate communities in high-risk landslide areas.

- Provide early warning systems for landslides and educate communities on evacuation procedures.

Remember, preparedness and awareness are key in minimizing the impact of hazards. Stay informed, follow official guidelines, and be proactive in taking necessary precautions.
