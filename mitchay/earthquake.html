Earthquakes: The Philippines is situated on the "Pacific Ring of Fire," a region prone to seismic activity. Earthquakes can occur due to the movement of tectonic plates, leading to ground shaking, landslides, and tsunamis.

Earthquakes:

- Conduct regular structural assessments and retrofit buildings to make them earthquake-resistant.

- Create emergency response plans and conduct drills to ensure preparedness.

- Identify safe areas for evacuation and designate assembly points.

- Raise awareness about earthquake safety and proper response actions.

Remember, preparedness and awareness are key in minimizing the impact of hazards. Stay informed, follow official guidelines, and be proactive in taking necessary precautions.
